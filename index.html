About the firm.

Geoleric Consultancy is a fully Kenyan owned company with a portfolio of prestigious projects across Kenya. We have been providing services, to our customers who value the highest levels of quality and safety expertise. Our Diverse portfolio encompasses land use planning services, Environmental Impact Assessment advisory/services, - Environmental Audit advisory/services, Topographical Mapping advisory/services, Architectural design advisory/services, Utility Mapping, Planning and Management, Electricity and Communication Network Planning and Development, Research, Training and Management, etc. No matter how challenging a project or how remote its location, chances are Geoleric Consultants can handle it. This is because we bring an unmatched combination of knowledge, skill, experience, and customer commitment to every job. Time and again our work has demonstrated that the only limits on human achievement are those that we place on ourselves.

Mision.

To deliver, quality and timely professional advisory and service in Physical Planning, Land Survey & Administration and to high professional standards.

Vision.

To be a top Consultancy firm offering practical solutions to: Land Use; Physical Planning, Land Survey & Administration and high professional standards.

Values.

At Geoleric, our core values are discipline, integrity, safety and success. These are set out in our Company Code of Ethics along with a practical set of mutual obligations for everyone across the Geoleric Company.
